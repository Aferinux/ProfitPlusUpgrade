FAQs

What is Forex?

Forex stands for Foreign Exchange and is the buying and selling of currencies in order to make a profit from the changes in their value. Forex is one of the largest segments of the global financial industry.

How Can I Earn Money Trading Forex?

You earn money in Forex due to the difference in exchange rates. The trade mechanism is simple: you buy, for example, 1 Euro for $ 1.2 and wait until the Euro rises to 1.3 dollars. Once this happens, you sell the Euro and get a profit of $0.1.

How do I Lower the Risk when Trading Forex?

As with all markets, the trader’s investment is at risk when making independent trades. It is important to understand this, and to think seriously about reducing the risk where possible in the Forex market.

However, professional and competent trader who wants to increase the ratio of return/ risk, would not be afraid to leave the market, limiting losses and abandon small profits. The use of this approach in practice can significantly increase income from trading in the Forex market.
